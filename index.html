Super Store Item Finder App

About

The Superstore Item Finder app was part of a project developted at BCIT in the Digital Design and Development program. It was a project done with a group I was in. Our group leader proposed this app idea to combat the struggles of finding items at the grocery store.

BCIT Video Project

About

In BCIT me and my classmates learned many things, one of which was video production and editing. For this project, my team and I had to create a fictional app and make a short video or advirtisment about this app and its features. We chose to do an app that focuses on helping tourists find attractions and places to go in the Greater Vancouver area.

Details

This project was challenging for me because it was the first time I tried to edit a video onto a green screan, normally it would be easy, but the angle in which it was done made it so there was a lot of tracking issues which I had to edit manually. Overall, this project was really fun, we went to a lot of different locations to shoot the different scenes.
